A complete list of the work, a one-line elevator pitch for each, and an honest read on how much real content exists today. This is also the document map for the new Sanity project: each row becomes a `caseStudy`, `project`, or `musing` document.

**Readiness key**

- ✅ **Drafted** — full case study written, ready to shape into the schema
- 🟡 **Framed** — real context and positioning exist, but no full narrative and no hard metrics
- 🟠 **Sketch** — partial or inferred from related material, needs a proper pass
- 🔴 **Name only** — I have the title and nothing else, needs a line from you

**Standing gap (applies to all Access / QikServe work):** no hard commercial metrics anywhere in the source material. Your own brief insists on quantified outcomes, so this is the single biggest blocker to credible case studies. Flagged per row.
